About

MISSION is a composer based in Los Angeles, crafting original music for the world's most ambitious brands. For the past four years, MISSION has collaborated closely with Concret Form across a wide range of projects — earning recognition at both the AICP Awards and the Shots Awards, including Bronze for Composition of the Year at the AICP Awards 2025 and Silver at the Shots Award.
